Some doors we have opened. This one remains closed—not because we failed, but because we have reached the edge of the map.

And perhaps that is as it should be. A cosmos with no mystery would be a cosmos fully computed—static, dead, complete. The open question is the breath of the living origin, still creating, still knowing, still becoming.
