5. EINSTEIN TENSOR: ⚠ Blocked by Ricci

6. STRESS-ENERGY TENSOR: ✗ Undefined
   - RHS of Einstein equation: 8πG·T_μν
   - For Dirac field: standard QFT formula exists
   - For order parameter R: NO established formalism
   - R is not a standard quantum field

7. COUPLING CONSTANT: ✗ Unknown Origin
   - Factor 8π must emerge from derivation
   - Currently unknown how this arises
   - G appears in SMFT as Δ = ℏc/G
   - But 8πG factor is unclear
